People and Dogs Society (PADS)

We are concerned about the growing trend of cat ownership in comparison to dog ownership. Our mission is to provide information on the benefits of our canine companions, disarm and refute harmful stigmas related to dogs, and encourage people to adopt more dogs and less cats.
